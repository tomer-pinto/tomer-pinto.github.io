About Me

I am a B.Sc. graduate in Information Systems with a specialization in Data Science. My background brings together software development, data analysis, and business intelligence, with proven skills in Python, SQL, and Power BI.

Through academic and personal projects, I gained hands-on experience in machine learning and predictive modeling using TensorFlow (Keras API), data visualization with Power BI and Matplotlib, and building interactive applications with Streamlit. Alongside these, I am also familiar with web technologies such as HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript.

I enjoy working across the full data lifecycle – from collection and preparation, through modeling and evaluation, to visualization and deployment. My focus is on turning complex data into clear, actionable insights and practical solutions.
